Before we get into the specifies of the 960 Grid System, let's briefly discuss grid-based system design in general. The idea is certainly not something that originated on the Web. In fact, it stems from one of the oldest and most basic design principles: alignment. Our brains like to simplify things to make them readily understandable. This is why we try to imposs order on things that seem chaotic, like seeing a face in the craters on the moon.
